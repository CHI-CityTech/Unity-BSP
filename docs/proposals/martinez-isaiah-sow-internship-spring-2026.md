# 1. Purpose of Internship

This engineering internship supports the development of foundational
infrastructure for a Unity-based digital twin of LG-38 (CHI Studios)
under the CHI meta-project framework. The internship is structured as a
scoped technical investigation and implementation effort intended to
establish reproducible modeling standards, asset governance protocols,
and CAD-to-engine translation workflows.

The internship does not aim to complete the full blended stage system.
Rather, it establishes the architectural and procedural groundwork
necessary for multi-student continuation and future expansion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Technical Scope

The intern will develop and validate a structured pipeline for
constructing a room-scale virtual environment aligned with physical
space. The work includes:

1.  Unity project architecture and render pipeline configuration.
2.  Modeling workflow evaluation (Unity-native vs external DCC vs
    hybrid).
3.  Geometric construction of an accurate digital twin shell of LG-38.
4.  Lighting approximation and PBR material workflow development.
5.  Asset naming, storage, and governance standards.
6.  Prototype AutoCAD-to-Unity import pipeline for partial TSlot
    configuration.
7.  Basic mechanical articulation proof-of-concept (hinged triptych
    pivot system).

The emphasis is on engineering validation, pipeline clarity, and
documentation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Work Phases and Deliverables

## Phase I – Project Architecture and Standards (Approx. 30 Hours)

Deliverables:

- Configured Unity project (render pipeline documented).
- Asset folder architecture and naming standard document.
- Modeling Workflow Comparison Memo with recommended CHI modeling standard.

Outcome: A stable and reproducible project scaffold suitable for
collaborative extension.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase II – LG-38 Geometric Digital Twin (Approx. 45 Hours)

Deliverables:

- Dimensionally validated LG-38 Unity scene (within defined tolerance).
- Coordinate origin and scale validation documentation.
- Baseline lighting configuration and summary memo.
- Limited LG-38 Material Kit for primary surfaces.

Outcome: A functional room-scale digital twin ready for staged insertion
of additional structures.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase III – CAD Translation Prototype (Approx. 25 Hours)

Deliverables:

- Documented AutoCAD-to-Unity translation protocol.
- Imported and correctly scaled partial TSlot prototype.
- Report on pipeline friction points and corrective recommendations.

Outcome: Validated CAD ingestion workflow for future stage expansion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase IV – Mechanical Articulation Proof-of-Concept (Approx. 10 Hours)

Deliverables:

- Hinged triptych pivot prototype.
- Pivot placement and constraint documentation.

Outcome: Validated approach to stage-mechanics representation inside
Unity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase V – Documentation Consolidation and Handoff (Approx. 10 Hours)

Deliverables:

- Consolidated Technical Documentation Package including:
   
- Project Architecture Guide
   
- Modeling Standard
   
- Asset Specification Checklist
   
- Material Pipeline Checklist
   
- CAD Translation Guide
   
- Roadmap for subsequent internship cycle.

Outcome: A documented and reproducible technical foundation ready for
continuation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Engineering Constraints and Assumptions

• Total effort limited to 120 hours. • Scope restricted to foundational
infrastructure, not full production. • Emphasis on geometric fidelity,
not photorealism. • CAD integration limited to prototype validation. •
No sensor integration or advanced behavioral systems included.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Evaluation Criteria

The internship will be evaluated on:

1.  Technical clarity and reproducibility of the Unity project
    architecture.
2.  Dimensional accuracy and runtime stability of the LG-38 digital
    twin.
3.  Successful validation of a CAD import workflow.
4.  Quality and completeness of engineering documentation.
5.  Practical scalability for future CHI contributors.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Alignment with CHI Engineering Objectives

This internship contributes to CHI by formalizing the translation
between physical architectural design and virtual representation within
a blended environment framework. The deliverable is an engineered
pipeline and documented system architecture capable of supporting future
multi-disciplinary collaboration and research expansion.

The primary output is therefore not a finished virtual stage, but a
validated technical foundation suitable for iterative development.
